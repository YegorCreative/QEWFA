Sponsor a Child

  Sponsoring a child is a way of directly helping a child that is dire need. Your help goes towards improving the physical emotional and social well-being of the child. For a child to know that they have someone who cares enough to help them in live give them the motivation to excel intellectual and become reputable leaders in society.

  When you become a sponsor you receive regular updates on the child’s progress in live from our local staff. You enable the child be part of community activities such as children’s learning events and community events.
